1998 Mark 4 Toyota Supra

One of the fastest cars to ever grace the earth. The Mark 4 Supra is a verhicle that can get from point A to B so fast, it will have you home before dinner is even ready. The Mark 4 Supra is a staple within the Japanese car scene, being featured in countless movies such as the Fast and Furious franchise. The interior is different from other cars, being driver focused you truly do feel like a pilot driving one of the best cars on the market.

1999 Nissan R34 Skyline GT-R

Another legend within the car scene is the legendary R34 by Nissan. This car is every car enthusiast dream car, being a car that has been and still is one of the most sought after vehicles of all time. This vehicle was especially propelled into popularity by the late Paul Walker who made this car one of the most iconic vehicles of all time.

1995 Mazda RX-7

This car features something that most other cars do not have, a rotary engine. Where instead of the usual pistons on a normal car, there is a sort of triangle that spins at varying RPMs to produce the monster-like power that the RX7 can make.

1985 Toyota AE-86

The AE-86 series of cars are small front/rear wheel drive models that have been most iconic in Japan. Being featured in anime such as Initial-D, the tofu sprinter or the AE-86 is a legend when it comes to cars. Being one of the most widely recognizable cars whether you watch anime or are into cars. This car is an iconic cultural symbol in Japanese culture and Western culture being some people's dream car even thought its close to 40 years old.
